A NEW ERA OF WATER POLO IN ORANGE COUNTY

Invictus Aquatics is a competitive water polo club dedicated to developing athletes of all levels. Founded in 2026, our program is rooted in discipline, teamwork, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Our mission is to cultivate the next generation of water polo players through elite coaching, competitive play, and a culture that demands the best from every athlete in the water.
