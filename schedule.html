04

POOL LOCATIONS

TRAINING FACILITIES IN ORANGE COUNTY

Invictus Aquatics trains at several pool facilities throughout Orange County, selected for their water polo-appropriate dimensions, availability, and convenience for families across the region. We rotate between facilities as needed to accommodate practice schedules and seasonal demand.

Our primary training pools are located in central and south Orange County, providing easy access for athletes from Irvine, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Mission Viejo, Laguna Niguel, Dana Point, San Clemente, and surrounding communities. Specific pool addresses and directions are provided to registered athletes before the start of each season.

All of our pool facilities feature competition-standard dimensions and water polo goal setups. For outdoor pool locations, athletes should come prepared with sunscreen and appropriate sun protection for daytime practices.
